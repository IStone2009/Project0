Ian Stone was born in New Bedford, MA. After moving to Boston, he spent several years as a music producer and DJ. Relocating to Los Angeles, he graduated from Citrus College with degrees in Recording Technology and Social Behavioral Sciences. Ian is currently working as an Audio Operator for Pixelogic Media, LLC handling client orders from the biggest entertainment companies in the world. His focus and goals are to continue pursuing personal professional goals within the audio industry.
